1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Mystic App ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our tarot reading application.

By using Mystic App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We recommend that you read this policy carefully before using the app.

Minimum age: This app is intended for users aged 13 or older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Voluntarily

When you use Mystic App, you may provide the following information:

  • Username: A name you choose to provide when generating a reading (optional)
  • Questions/Prompts: The text you write when requesting a tarot reading
  • Saved readings: Reading history you choose to save locally

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Unique user identifier (UUID): A unique code generated automatically on first use and stored locally on your device. This UUID is not linked to your real identity.
  • App usage data: Information about how you interact with the app (selected cards, number of readings performed)
  • Technical data: Information about your device, OS version, and app version

2.3 Third-Party Information

Google AdMob (Rewarded Ads)

  • Device advertising ID (IDFA/AAID)
  • IP address
  • Ad interaction data
  • Device and browser information
  • Approximate geolocation data

Supabase (Backend and Storage)

  • IP address (server logs)
  • HTTP request data
  • Reward validation information

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Purposes

  • Generate personalized tarot readings: Your username and question are sent to OpenAI to generate personalized interpretations
  • Validate ad rewards: UUID and reading-related data are used to verify you watched an ad before generating a reading
  • Store readings: Your readings are saved locally on your device and temporarily on our server during the validation process
  • Show relevant ads: Google AdMob may use data to personalize ads shown to you
  • Improve the app: We analyze aggregated and anonymized data to improve user experience

3.2 Legal Basis (LGPD/GDPR)

  • Consent: By using the app and accepting this policy, you consent to the processing of your data
  • Performance of a contract: Processing is necessary to provide the service you request (tarot readings)
  • Legitimate interests: Preventing fraud and improving our services

4. Sharing of Information

4.1 Third-Party Services

OpenAI (AI Reading Generation)

  • Data shared: Username, question/prompt, selected cards
  • Purpose: Generate personalized tarot interpretations using artificial intelligence
  • Retention: OpenAI retains data according to its own privacy policy
  • Policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

Supabase (Backend and Database)

  • Data shared: UUID, username, question, reading ID, reward validation data
  • Purpose: Validate that ads were watched before processing readings
  • Data location: Servers in the United States (West US - Oregon)
  • Retention: Validation data is kept for 5 minutes and then expires automatically
  • Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

Google AdMob (Rewarded Ads)

  • Data shared: Advertising ID, device information, interaction data
  • Purpose: Show rewarded ads and validate views
  • Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Ad personalization: You can disable personalized ads in your device settings

4.2 International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed on servers located outside of Brazil, including:

  • United States (Supabase, OpenAI, Google)

We ensure these transfers are carried out with appropriate safeguards, including standard contractual clauses and GDPR compliance.

4.3 Legal Disclosures

We may disclose your information if required:

  • By law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • To prevent fraud or illegal activity

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local Storage

  • Saved readings: Stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences (encrypted by the operating system)
  • UUID: Stored locally on your device
  • Control: You can delete saved readings anytime within the app or by uninstalling the app

5.2 Server Storage

  • Validation data: Stored temporarily in Supabase for up to 5 minutes to validate rewards
  • Security: Data is transmitted via HTTPS/TLS, database protected with Row Level Security (RLS)

5.3 Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period Location
Saved readings Until you delete them or uninstall the app Local device
Reward validation data 5 minutes Supabase server
Server logs 30 days Supabase server
OpenAI data As per OpenAI policy OpenAI servers
AdMob data As per Google policy Google servers
